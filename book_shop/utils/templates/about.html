About This Project

You arrived at the stunning website made to bring back the beauty of literature to the masses. You will be easily capable of searching for any book in our database, searching for their reviews, authors, and many more.

About Me

I am a Data Science student at the Warsaw University of Technology (WUT). This project is developed as part of my classes to apply the knowledge I've gained and to create a useful resource for book lovers. Through this website, I aim to combine my passion for literature with data science by providing an easy-to-use platform for exploring books and their reviews.
